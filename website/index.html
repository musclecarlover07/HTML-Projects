This website is a collection of some of the top football leagues in the world. It displays the team name, where the team is located. As well as their stadium and capacity.

Each league consists of 20 teams with the exception to the Bundesliga, which consists of only 18 teams. Each year teams will be relegated to the lower league and an equal amount of teams will be promoted. All teams are current as of May 22, 2018. Some league will have a record or 2 showing "TBD". Those teams have not be determined yet.

This website is used for informational use. It can be seen as educational. Anyone who is a football fan, especially of the currently chosen leagues, will enjoy this website.

League Information

Each league consists of 20 teams with the exception of the Bundesliga, which consists of 18 teams. Each year 3 teams will be relegated, or demoted to the league in a lower tier. Then 3 teams are promoted to the top league. Usually the teams that finish 1st and 2nd teams in the second league are automatically promoted. Then 3rd place to 6th place have a playoff tournament to see who is the 3rd team to qualify.
